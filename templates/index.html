Discord Bot

MyAnimeRec is also available as a Discord Bot. Click the 'Invite Me' button to add it to your server!

The bot supports the following commands:

  • !rec <username> - Get 10 recommendations for a MAL username.
  • !rec <username> <username>... - Get 10 recommendations for multiple usernames simultaneously (no limit).
  • p!rec <username>... - Receive the recommendations privately.

About

Hello everyone! My name is Mehdi and I am a college student who is passionate about Data Science and Anime.

As you can probably tell, my favorite anime character is.. Luffy from One Piece. I connect with him on so many levels because of the values and ideals he embodies. From his pursuit of freedom to his determination and perseverance, and how he values friendship and loyalty. I strive to become like him, spread kindness around me, and enjoy the simple things in life.

I made this project because MAL only has a recommendation system based on user contribution, which I think is hard to navigate. I also wanted to practice my coding skills.
MyAnimeRec also allows you to get common recommendations for an unlimited number of users (if you want to organize a watch party)

Currently, it is running on a simple system based on the similarity of genres and synopsis. (More details in the Github repo). My goal with MyAnimeRec is to progressively improve it as we gain more users, and move to a collaborative filtering approach or a more advanced content-based system (or both).
